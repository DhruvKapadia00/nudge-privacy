Nudge: Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

The short version

Nudge does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

Nudge stores all your tasks, completion history, and preferences locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have servers. We do not have accounts. We do not have analytics. We cannot see your data.

No accounts required

Nudge does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

No tracking

Nudge does not use any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or third-party services that collect user data.

No network requests

Nudge works entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests. Your data never leaves your device.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Nudge sends local notifications to remind you to check in. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent through any external server.

Data deletion

To delete all your Nudge data, simply delete the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, deleting the app permanently removes everything.

Children's privacy

Nudge does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the date above. Given that we collect no data, changes are unlikely.
